What's eurucamp?

Just like you, we noticed a huge amount of interest in EuRuKo this year and a lot of people (including the organisers of eurucamp) weren't able to get tickets.

So we're assembling an alternative programme for those who want to come into Berlin and hear about, talk about and hack about (with) Ruby.

What we're planning on doing:

  • A Barcamp-style event consisting of workshops and hack-spaces.
  • Depending on interest, a alternative line up of talks.

As far as possible, we'll coordinate the social events with the EuRuKo organisers, so you'll still have a chance to meet up with those attending.
